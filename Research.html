Our Organization

The University of Ghana is one of the Africa's most distinguished public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound. whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. University Of Ghana educates more than 38,000 students annually, with approximately 2.64% being computer science students. We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us.

Our Community

If you are enthused by a big challenge, you've come to the right place! The computer science community at the university of Ghana encourages it's people to be vibrant and brave in taking on new challenges that push boundaries beyond imagination.

Building Our Future

So, what defines our students, faculty and community members? Above all, it is our belief in the infinite potentials and our unshakable optimism. It is a connection to others near and far. A hunger for socio-economic transformation that pushes us to tackle challenges and hound progress. It is the conviction that together we can create a world of good. Join us on the journey.
